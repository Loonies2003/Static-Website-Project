The basics

There are seven major muscle groups for which one exercise will be demonstrated for each:

1. Chest

The best exercise to develop your chest is the classic barbell bench press. A simple but effective movement if done correctly. Focus on your form and try to go as heavy as you can on this exercise.

2. Shoulders

For shoulders, we have to consider the fact that it has three parts. Dumbell shoulder press for the front delts, lateral raises for the side delts and reverse flys for the reardelts

3. Triceps

If you want your arms to look big, you should definitely focus on your triceps as they make up about 70% of your arm mass! The most effective exercise to work your triceps would be barbell skullcrushers but it is a dificult exercise to learn so i would advise sticking with tricep extensions.

4. Back

If you want V-Taper physique, training your back is essential. The most effective exercise is the Lat pulldown. Try and keep your elbows tucked in close and try and keep our thumbs above the bar as this engages your back more instead of your biceps.

5. Biceps

The simplest muscle group out of all. The best exercise? Curls. All types of curls, barbell curls, dumbell curls, hammer curls any type of bicep curl is effective.

6. Quads

No one wants to be called chicken legs. That is why the best exercise is the good old barbell squat. Go heavy on these but try to keep your form in check.

7. Hamstrings

If you want to have explosivenes in your athletics as well as be able to jump high, you should definitely incorporate Barbell Romanian Deadlifts into your workout. Yes, they are a pain and will literally leave you feeling "dead", but that is the fun off it.
