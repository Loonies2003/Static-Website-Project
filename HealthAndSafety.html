Workout Safety tips

1) Stay hydrated:

Stay hydrated. A no-brainer, right? You get hot and sweaty at the gym and you need water to replenish what you sweat out.

2) Warm up first:

Warm-up exercises increase blood flow to muscles, raise body temperature, and prepare the body for physical activity.

3) Know your body:

Listening to your body before, during, and after your exercise routine can help prevent injuries. Having sore muscles after exercising is to be expected.

4) Adequate Rest and Recovery:

Incorporate rest and recovery periods into the training program. Overtraining can lead to fatigue, compromised form, and an increased risk of injuries.
