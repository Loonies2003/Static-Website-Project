My story:

Hi, my name is Talhah Loonat and this is my story of how i started to workout and how i have made it my passion and how I would like to inspire people to start working out.

Ever since i was young, i was always mocked at for being skinny and underweight. People would mock me call me insulting names and this lowered my self-esteem.

In 2020, due to the lockdown enforced by the Government because of the Corona virus outbreak, I decided to start working out at home with some old dumbells that my brother had.

I did not know anything about working out, and i would just do random exercises throughout the day hoping that it would make me gain weight. I knew nothing about a healthy diet and bulking.

As weeks went by, I started to watch a YouTuber called David Laid.He inspired me and to workout everyday and it was through him that I learnt how to bulk and all the different exercises and he showed me that nothing is impossible and that nothing comes to you easy, you have to work for it.

Whenever I have no motivation to workout anymore, I watch this specific video which inspires me and motivates me alot. I wish to one day, replicate this video and to also inspire others that nothing is impossible.
