Food is Fuel

The 30/70 rule is a concept that says that to get into shape and to stay fit, it is based off thirty percent of working out and training and seventy percent on what you put into your body

Your body need protein to repair its muscles after you have worked out and to help them recover in time for the next workout. Your body also relies on heavily carbohydrates for energy.

This is a basic breakdown of the different food sources available for portein, carbohydrates and fats.

  • Protein
    1. Salmon
    2. Chicken
    3. Eggs
    4. Red Meat
    5. Processed Meats
  • Carbohydrates
    1. Brown rice
    2. Fresh fruit
    3. Dried fruit
    4. White rice
    5. Sugary cereals
  • Fats
    1. Avocados
    2. Olive oil
    3. Egg yolks
    4. Coconut oil
    5. Butter
